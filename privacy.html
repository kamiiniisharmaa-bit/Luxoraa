Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Luxoraa

1. Our commitment to privacy

At Luxoraa, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our promise: We never sell your personal data. Your trust is our most valued asset.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address.
  • Payment information: Credit card details (processed securely via our payment partners).
  • Usage data: Browsing behavior, IP address, device type, and interaction with our site.
  • Preferences: Product interests, wishlist items, and communication preferences.

3. How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders, including sending order confirmations and updates.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Personalize your shopping experience and recommend products you may like.
  • Send promotional content (only if you opt-in) and inform you about new collections or sales.
  • Improve our website, products, and services through analytics and feedback.

4. Data protection & security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All sensitive information (such as payment details) is encrypted using SSL technology. We also restrict access to personal data to authorized personnel only.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and tracking

Luxoraa uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our site. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling cookies may affect some functionalities.

You can opt out of non-essential cookies at any time. We respect your choices and are transparent about our data practices.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, and delivering orders. These parties are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@luxoraa.com.

8. Children's privacy

Luxoraa does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

This version of the Privacy Policy is effective as of June 17, 2026. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to our support team.
